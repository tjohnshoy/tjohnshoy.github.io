User Research

We began our research by interviewing six individuals that were selected based on the established proto persona. The interview script was aimed at finding information on the steps people take while trip planning and the priorities they have for their vacations. Survey data was collected from 29 respondents. The key demographics were people working full time, who traveled 1-3 times per year.



Definition

Travel apps and websites have been designed to help travelers plan the details of their vacations; However, we have observed that travelers find planning all the details of their vacation to be cumbersome, and would like to have some spontaneous experiences. How might we reduce the pre-planning workload and empower travelers to be spontaneous while they are on-the-go in their travel destination?

Ideation

After compiling and reviewing the potential features for the app, two main features were determined to best fit the needs of the user: An itinerary builder and library, and a review system which allows for user endorsement

User Testing

The objective for my guerilla testing was to determine if users can successfully create a trip, find the favorites section, add an itinerary to their trip, and apply filters to their search. I tested these tasks as they are the primary function of the app, and the usability of these features is key to ensuring users will repeatedly use the app for travel.

Final Thoughts

When we began researching for this project, I was surprised to find that the average user values and enjoys some spontaneity while they are traveling. Based on the research, I believe people will continue to migrate towards a more spontaneous mode of travelling since smartphones put the power in the users hands to make decisions in the moment.

Although this app addresses a user need, there is a great deal of opportunity to expand the offerings in the future. A few such opportunities include integration with ride sharing apps like Lyft and Uber, purchasing capabilities, and personality profiles to enable user suggestions.
